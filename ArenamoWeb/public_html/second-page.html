Goregaon Sports Club

Goregaon Sports Club, commonly known as GSC, is located on the Goregaon-Malad Link road, half way between Inorbit Mall and Infinity Mall in Malad. While the facility is open to members only, non-members can avail certain facilities if accompanied by or as a guest of a registered member of the club. GSC is one of the largest multi-purpose sports facilities in the city. The club has an olympic-size swimming pool, a fully equipped gymnasium with cardio and weight training equipment, and six acres of outdoor facilities for cricket, football, volleyball, basketball and tennis. The outdoor facilities are largely on a first-come first-serve basis, apart from when they have been reserved for members, coaching sessions or leased out for competitive tournaments.The club also has indoor facilities for badminton, squash, table tennis, skating, and an air rifle shooting range. In addition to the sports facilities, the club also has rooms for indoor games including billiards, snooker, chess and carrom. The facilities are largely well maintained, however membership is the main criteria for access to most facilities and services, which include a variety of additional benefits such as banquet halls for hire. Different membership packages are available and the costs vary depending on the age of the applicant and various other factors, the details for which are available with the facility management. The club is open on all days except Wednesday.
